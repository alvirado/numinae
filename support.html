Frequently Asked Questions

How does Numinae calculate my numbers?
Numinae uses the traditional Arcana numerological system. Your name is converted to numeric values using a classical esoteric letter table, and your birthdate provides the temporal foundation. These calculate five distinct numbers: Personality, Family, Projects, Reflection, and Transcendence.
Is my personal data (name, birthdate) shared with anyone?
Never. All data you enter — your name, birthdate, and your beloved's information — is stored exclusively on your device. It is never uploaded, shared, or accessible to us or any third party. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all your data.
Why does the app show ads?
Ads allow us to provide Numinae as a free app. We've carefully designed ad placement to minimize disruption — ads appear only after you've finished reading your results, never during the experience itself. The app-open ad only triggers when returning to the app from the background, never on cold launch.
My Karmic Resonance data disappeared after restarting the app.
This was a known issue in early versions. Starting from version 1.1.0, the beloved's name and birthdate are automatically saved and restored when you reopen the Karmic Resonance screen. Make sure you have the latest version installed.
What do the Roman numerals mean?
Each of your five arcana numbers is displayed in Roman numerals (I through XXII) to reflect the Tarot-Arcana tradition that this numerological system is rooted in. For example, VII represents the seventh arcana — Victory and Astral Light.
How do I switch between English and Spanish?
Tap the globe icon (🌐 EN/ES) in the top-right corner of the main screen. The app will instantly translate all content including interpretations, labels, and daily energy readings.
How do I change my Information or my Beloved's Information?
For your own data: tap "Life Path" from the main menu — your name and birthdate are editable there. For your beloved's information: go to "Karmic Resonance" and tap the "Change" (Cambiar) button inside the results view to start a new analysis.
Is Numinae available on Android?
The Android version of Numinae is currently in development. You can register your interest by contacting us at the email below, and we'll notify you when it launches.
What does the "Daily Notification" do?
If you grant notification permission, Numinae sends you a gentle morning reminder to check your daily energy. This notification is scheduled daily at 8:00 AM local time. You can disable it at any time in your device's notification settings for Numinae.
